***TD\_BeeperDeLuxe***  
Features:  

- sound a beep tone
- vibrate the phone
- sound and vibrate
- sound intermitting alarmtone

  
Properties:  
(Beeper)Duration (Miöllsecond) the duration of the sound.  
Freq the frequency of the beep  
Alarmduration duration in Millisconds.  
  
To use you need to **implement libs as followes**:  

- core
- xui
- phone
- audio
